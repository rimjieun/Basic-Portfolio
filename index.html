I'm Jieun Rim, a full stack web developer currently located in Atlanta, GA looking to work with a passionate team of developers. I love understanding how things work and using technology to help people solve their problems. I work across the full MERN stack and have a strong interest in civic tech and AR.

I like participating in civic hackathons to challenge myself, to gain more team experience, and to learn how developers can contribute to communities.

The MARTA Hackathon—a Code for Atlanta event—was my very first hackathon, and it was an awesome experience. Our team's project was to create a 3D overview of MARTA bus movements and traffic flow in the North Avenue-Midtown area using real-time data and CesiumJS.
